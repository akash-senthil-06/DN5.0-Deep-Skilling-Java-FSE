# Exercise 1: Control Structures

## Objective
To understand PL/SQL control flow mechanisms (loops, conditional checks like IF-THEN-ELSE) by implementing business operations on loans and customer statuses.

## Scenarios
1. **Senior Interest Discount**: Loops through customers, calculates age, and applies a 1% discount to loan interest rates for those over 60.
2. **VIP Promotion**: Sets `IsVIP` to `'TRUE'` for customers holding a balance over $10,000.
3. **Due Reminders**: Fetches loans due within 30 days and logs reminder alerts.
